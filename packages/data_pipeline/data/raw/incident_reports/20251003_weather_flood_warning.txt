EVENT REPORT - WEATHER ADVISORY

Date: 2025-10-03 08:00:00
Region: SoCal
Type: Weather
Affected Routes: Highway 101, Pacific Coast Highway (PCH)

Details:
Heavy rainfall overnight has caused significant flooding along the Pacific Coast Highway between Malibu and Santa Monica. The highway is currently closed to all commercial traffic.
Additionally, a rockslide has been reported on Highway 101 near the Conejo Grade, causing major delays for all northbound traffic.
All vehicles scheduled for deliveries in the coastal region must be rerouted via I-5 and local inland roads. Expect delays of at least 90 minutes for all affected shipments.
